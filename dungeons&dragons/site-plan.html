Overview

Purpose

Dungeons and Dragons is the number one Tabletop Role Playing Game in the World. Learn about Dungeons and Dragons and see a sneak peak on what it's like and what you can do with it! Prepare to feel immersed in this experience as you get transported from wherever your'e seeing this and into the multiverse known as Dungeons & Dragons.

Audience

Anyone can play! Wether your'e 80, 90, 12 etc. There is room for all to enjoy, all to learn, and all to compete for glory, fame, and fortune.
